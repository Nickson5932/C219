Parts of a Web Page [Paulo]

Header

The top part of a website containing the Logo and usually the site's navigation menu.

Navigation

Links at the top of the page to help you find what you are looking for.It mostly appears in page headers or sidebars.

Banner

It is a large image you see at the top of a web page that grabs your attention. For example to publicize products or services.

Content

All the elements used to communicate ypur message - text, images, audio, video, etc.


Footer

Performs the same function as the header except that footer is at the bottom of the page instead. They can contain contact info, privacy policy, sitemap and other important pages in the website.
